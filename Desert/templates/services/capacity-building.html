Capacity Building

Key Components of Our Procurement Capacity Building and Training Services:

Institutional Skills Development:We design and deliver training programs focused on strengthening the procurement competencies of government agencies, PIUs, and donor-funded institutions. Topics include public procurement laws, tendering processes, and contract management, tailored to align with both national and international standards.

Training for Tender Committees & Evaluation Panels:Our workshops equip tender committee members with the skills to manage procurement processes transparently and efficiently. We cover evaluation techniques, risk mitigation in supplier selection, and ethical standards in procurement.

Procurement Planning & Budgeting Workshops:We guide participants through the full cycle of procurement planning — from needs assessment and market analysis to procurement method selection and budgeting. Participants gain tools to draft realistic, fundable procurement plans.

Benefits of Choosing Desert Storm for Procurement Capacity Building:

Improved Procurement Outcomes:Well-trained teams reduce procurement delays, errors, and compliance risks — leading to more efficient and value-driven procurement.

Donor & Stakeholder Confidence:Demonstrating trained and competent procurement teams enhances your institution’s credibility with funding agencies and international stakeholders.

Sustainable Institutional Growth:Our training programs embed long-term procurement best practices within institutions, reducing reliance on external consultants and enhancing autonomy.
