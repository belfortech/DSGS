Public Procurement Strategy and Advisory

Key Components of Our Public Procurement Strategy & Advisory Services:

Procurement Strategy Development:We collaborate with Ministries, Departments, and Agencies (MDAs) to create custom procurement strategies that promote value-for-money, efficiency, and alignment with national development goals.

Regulatory Alignment & Compliance:Our experts ensure procurement policies and frameworks meet both local Public Procurement Acts and international donor regulations, minimizing risks and ensuring transparent processes.
