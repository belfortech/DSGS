Procurement Audit

Key Components of Our Procurement Audit Services:

Regulatory & Policy Compliance:We assess alignment with applicable national procurement laws, donor procurement frameworks (e.g., World Bank, USAID, UN), and institutional policies.

Process Integrity & Transparency:Our audits evaluate whether procurement processes—such as tendering, evaluation, and contract award—are conducted fairly, competitively, and transparently.

Risk Identification & Mitigation:We identify procurement risks including fraud, conflict of interest, weak internal controls, and recommend practical mitigation strategies.

Contract Management Audit:We review how contracts are managed post-award—including delivery, payment, variations, and performance monitoring—to ensure obligations are fulfilled efficiently.

Why Choose DSGS for Your Procurement Audit?

Independence & Objectivity:As a trusted third party, we offer impartial insights that foster accountability and improved decision-making.

Expertise in Public & Donor-Funded Procurement:Our auditors are well-versed in government frameworks and international donor standards, ensuring relevance across sectors and regions.

Confidentiality & Professional Integrity:We uphold the highest standards of confidentiality, ethics, and professionalism throughout the audit process.
