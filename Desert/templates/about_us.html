Who we are

Company overview

Desert Storm Global Sourcing & Procurement Consultants stands as a leading procurement and business consultancy firm catering to Government Institutions and internationally funded organizations. With its headquarters situated in Dubai, our firm strategically positions itself to serve as a vital link connecting Africa and Arab countries.

At Desert Storm, we understand the critical role that procurement plays in shaping the success of businesses across diverse industries. Our team of seasoned experts brings a wealth of experience and a global perspective to the table, ensuring that our clients receive tailored, innovative, and sustainable solutions. We take pride in being at the forefront of driving efficiency and effectiveness in procurement processes, empowering our clients to navigate the complexities of the international business landscape with confidence.

Key pillars of our expertise include PMS Strategy Development and Execution, where we collaboratively work with clients to design and implement robust procurement strategies aligned with their organizational goals. Additionally, our proficiency in Stock Control, Monitoring, Warehousing, and Distribution ensures optimal supply chain management, minimizing risks and enhancing overall operational efficiency.

Business Process Re-engineering is a core competency at Desert Storm, where we analyze, optimize, and streamline existing procurement processes to unlock greater value. Our PSM Planning, Forecasting, and Risk Assessment services provide clients with the foresight needed to make informed decisions, mitigate potential challenges, and capitalize on emerging opportunities in the market.

We take pride in our ability to identify and nurture strategic opportunities that allow our clients to expand their global footprint and thrive in the dynamic business landscape. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our client-centric approach, attention to detail, and consistent delivery of measurable results.

As a trusted partner in procurement and business consultancy, Desert Storm Global Sourcing & Procurement Consultants is poised to propel your organization towards success by optimizing processes, mitigating risks, and unlocking new avenues for growth.
